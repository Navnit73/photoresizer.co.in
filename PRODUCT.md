# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users
Everyday users who need quick image editing without installing software. Includes people preparing official document photos (passport, visa, ID), students, job applicants, and professionals needing quick compression, cropping, resizing, and background changes. Context is often urgent and search-driven ("resize my photo to 50KB"), requiring fast results and clear instructions.

## Product Purpose
To provide fast, simple, browser-based image editing utilities (resizing, cropping, background removal, formatting). It balances a powerful image editor with extreme simplicity, allowing first-time users to understand what to do within 5 seconds. The flow is clarity → upload → edit → download with minimum friction.

## Brand Personality
Simple, Reliable, Smart.
Clean like Canva, fast and utility-focused like TinyPNG, trustworthy like an official document service. Modern but not overly "AI futuristic".

## Anti-references
- Overly complex professional editor interfaces like Photoshop
- Too many panels, sliders, and confusing controls
- Dark/heavy AI-looking designs
- Generic SaaS landing pages with too much decoration
- Fake glossy gradients everywhere
- Ads-heavy free tool websites
- Cluttered UI with many competing buttons
- Old online converter website vibes

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over features:** Extreme simplicity so users understand what to do in 5 seconds.
- **Minimum friction:** The flow is upload → edit → download. Remove unnecessary steps.
- **Premium trust:** Clean, reliable design that feels like an official document service, not a cheap converter.
- **Fast utility:** Performance and clarity drive the design, avoiding heavy or overly decorated elements.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Clear readable typography with strong color contrast
- Large touch-friendly buttons
- Simple language for non-technical users
- Keyboard-friendly interactions
- Follow WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines as a target
- SEO-friendly semantic HTML, clear headings, and fast Core Web Vitals performance
